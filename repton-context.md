COMPANY OVERVIEW
Name: Repton Digital
Short name / logo: RPT. or RPT.DIGITAL
Type: SaaS & Web Development Agency
Location: Perth, Western Australia, Australia
Founded: 2025
Stage: Early-stage, founder-led, actively acquiring clients
Founder: Corey (16 years old, Year 11)
Repton Digital is a Perth-based SaaS and web development agency that builds custom websites and software products for businesses — from simple sites for local tradies to full SaaS products for founders with an idea. The agency is lean, technical, and founder-led, with a focus on real outcomes: online presence that converts, software that solves real problems, and products that scale.

DESIGN NOTES FOR DEVELOPERS

The brand is dark-first. Light mode is not a priority.
Glass UI is non-negotiable — every card, nav, and modal uses the liquid glass treatment.
The site should feel like something Apple would build if they were a web agency.
Animations should be smooth, purposeful, and premium — not flashy for the sake of it.
Mobile must be excellent. The target audience (tradies) often browse on phone.
No stock photos. Use geometric/abstract 3D visuals, gradients, and UI elements only.
The word "Repton" in text should always be followed by "Digital" — never standalone.
"RPT." or "RPT.DIGITAL" is acceptable as a condensed logo mark.
Accent colors: sky blue (#0ea5e9) primary, soft indigo (#818cf8) secondary.
Never use purple-on-white gradients — it's overdone in tech design.
The founder is 16. The site should NOT feel like it was built by a 16-year-old. It should feel like it was built by a world-class agency.
